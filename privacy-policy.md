# Privacy Policy for Roll Sketch

**Effective date: July 7, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application **Roll Sketch** ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

You can use most of the App — drawing, saving sketches locally, adjusting settings, and creating time-lapse recordings — **without creating an account**. Some optional features (such as syncing your profile and unlocking additional content) require you to sign in.

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Account information
When you choose to sign in, we use **Sign in with Apple** or **Google Sign-In**. Through these services we receive an account identifier and, depending on your settings, an email address (Apple may provide a private relay email). We use this only to create and authenticate your account.

### b. Profile information
If you create a profile, we store the information you provide:
- a username (public handle),
- an optional display name, and
- an avatar image — either an automatically generated identicon or a photo you choose to upload.

### c. User content
Sketches you save, and any time-lapse recordings you create, may be stored so they can be synced to your account. You control whether a saved drawing is private or shared.

### d. Photo library access
With your permission, the App accesses your device's photo library to:
- let you select a photo for your profile avatar, and
- save exported time-lapse GIFs to your library.

We only access photos you explicitly select or save. We do not scan or upload your photo library.

### e. On-device settings
Preferences such as pen sensitivity, dot size, language, and custom colors are stored **locally on your device** and are not transmitted to us.

### f. Information we do NOT collect
The App contains **no advertising, no third-party analytics, and no tracking SDKs**. We do not track you across other apps or websites.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information above to:
- authenticate you and maintain your account,
- display and sync your profile and saved content,
- provide the features you request (e.g., saving GIFs, choosing an avatar), and
- keep the service secure and functioning.

We do **not** sell your personal information.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third parties, each with its own privacy policy:

- **Supabase** — hosts our backend, authentication, database, and file storage for accounts, profiles, and synced content.
- **Apple (Sign in with Apple)** — authentication.
- **Google (Google Sign-In)** — authentication.
- **DiceBear (api.dicebear.com)** — generates default avatar images. Your device requests these images directly, which may expose your IP address to the service.
- **Google Fonts (fonts.gstatic.com)** — supplies a display font. Your device may fetch the font, which may expose your IP address to the service.

## 4. Sharing and Public Content

We share your information only:
- with the service providers listed above, to operate the App;
- content you choose to make public or share (such as a drawing you explicitly share); and
- where required by law.

Information you make public (for example, a username or a shared drawing) may be visible to other users.

## 5. Data Retention and Account Deletion

We keep your account data while your account is active. You can **delete your account at any time** from **Settings → Account → Delete Account**. Deleting your account removes your profile and associated content from our systems. Content stored only on your device is removed when you delete the App.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age required by your local law (for example, 13 in the U.S.). We do not knowingly collect personal information from such children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us so we can remove it.

## 7. Security

We use industry-standard measures, including encrypted (HTTPS) connections, to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective date" above. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
